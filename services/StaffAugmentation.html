Staffing at Client Site

SinnoFlex Staff Augmentation services provide skilled personnel to work under your direction to help you develop, maintain, manage and support your applications. Our skilled application professionals can help you manage fluctuating skill needs, skills gaps and changing staffing needs to meet your aggressive project timelines. Our resources work with you on-site or remote, and are certified in key technologies.

Staffing at Our Offshore Center

A conventional method of offshore model. Sinnoflex builds teams in its focus areas of work, which will remain with us at the end of the project. This model is suitable for companies where IT is non-core, and who are looking for short-term cost-savings on non-critical work. - We build a team for you that remain with us during project execution. - Team size will expand or contract depends on project requirement - We provide Resources and Standard Infrastructure for the team to function. - Client project managers and team leaders will manage the team and planning of deliverables. - This model requires minimal commitment of cost and no risk of employment

Build Operate and Transfer Development Center

This model is most recommended where the client has a need for extended teams and the requirement is medium or long-term. Suitable for companies wanting to own the center some time and are today unsure of offshore plans.

- We build a team for you, manage and transfer them at the end of the contractual period.

- We provide the standard Infrastructure and create new facilities during the transfer.

We offer better IP protection and increased security on sensitive issues.

Build Operate and Transfer Development Center

SinnoFlex Offshore development center at Kolkata, India, offers Build Operate and Transfer service to customers worldwide.
